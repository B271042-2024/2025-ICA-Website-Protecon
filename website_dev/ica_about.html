Prote-Con

Prote-Con acts as an integrated website, combining multiple tools for protein conservation explorations. Our objective is to expedite protein sequence analysis, by providing all-in-one feature. Tools: ClustalO, EMBOSS patmatmotifs, EMBOSS plotcon and IQTREE. Data retrieved via Prote-Con are from NCBI Protein database.
